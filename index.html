Biography

Hi! I am Chen Wang. I am interested in 2D image processing and 3D face reconstruction by using deep learning methodology.

Currently, I am working on progressive image inpainting tasks with a multi-model approach. With the translation from 2D to 3D, I am also curious to restore a occluded 3D face.
